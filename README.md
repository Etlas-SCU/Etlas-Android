# Etlas App
Etlas is a cutting-edge solution designed to revolutionize tourism experiences through its integrated mobile app and website. This README provides an overview of the Etlas app and website, highlighting their unique features and functionalities that cater to the diverse needs of travelers.

## Overview
The Etlas mobile app offers an immersive and informative experience, allowing users to explore a rich collection of tours, articles, and interactive 3D models of famous monuments and statues. By leveraging advanced recognition technology, users can scan monuments and statues, instantly accessing detailed information and viewing captivating 3D models. The ability to add models and articles to favorites enables personalized content curation, ensuring that users can easily access and revisit their preferred resources.

To further engage users, the app features an engaging knowledge game that tests their knowledge of monuments, Egyptian history, and more. This interactive game adds an element of fun and challenges users to expand their knowledge while enjoying the app's immersive content.

Complementing the mobile app, the Etlas website offers a comprehensive platform for trip planning, destination research, and exploration. Users can access a wealth of tours, articles, and other resources to enhance their understanding of various destinations. The website serves as a hub for users to discover, learn, and engage with the rich cultural and historical heritage of different regions.
